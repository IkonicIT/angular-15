1.To Merge Locations, select the Location you want to Merge from in the first location selector below.
2.Then select the Location you would like to Merge into in the second selector.
* Pressing 'Merge' will commit your changes. Remember:
1.All child locations of the first location will now be children of the second location.
2.All items located at the first location will now be located at the second location.
3.The first location will be deleted.
* Pressing 'Cancel' will bring you back to the Manage Locations screen.
